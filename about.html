Our Story

Founded in 2010, Afyanex Medical Centre began as a small clinic with a big vision - to make quality healthcare accessible to all residents of Bungoma and surrounding communities. Our founder, Dr. Jesse Gitaka, envisioned a medical facility that would provide comprehensive care with compassion and excellence.

Our Growth

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a full-service medical facility with modern equipment and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals. Our expansion has always been driven by community needs, and today we offer a wide range of medical services from general consultations to specialized care.
